What We Do

Research Focus

We investigate the molecular mechanisms of genomic instability in neurons and its link to neurodegenerative diseases.

🔬

PARP1 & PARP Inhibitors

The PARP1-TRIM44-MRN loop governs the cellular response to PARP inhibitors — a key axis in DNA damage signaling and therapeutic targeting.

💧

Liquid-Liquid Phase Separation

USP39 promotes NHEJ repair by poly(ADP-ribose)-induced liquid demixing, revealing how phase separation controls DNA repair fidelity.

🧬

Ubiquitin Modifiers & NGI

Ubiquitin and ubiquitin-like modifiers play critical roles in regulating neuronal genomic instability, linking proteostasis to genome integrity.

🧠

Neurodegeneration

We explore how unresolved DNA damage in neurons initiates and accelerates neurodegenerative diseases, aiming to identify early biomarkers and drug targets.
