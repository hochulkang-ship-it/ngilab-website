What We Study

Research Focus

01

PARP1–TRIM44–MRN Axis

The PARP1-TRIM44-MRN loop dictates the cellular response to PARP inhibitors — a key pathway in DNA damage signaling and precision oncology.

DNA Damage Response
02

Liquid–Liquid Phase Separation

USP39 drives NHEJ DNA repair via poly(ADP-ribose)-induced liquid demixing, revealing phase separation as a regulator of genome integrity.

Phase Separation
03

Ubiquitin Modifiers & NGI

Ubiquitin and ubiquitin-like modifiers regulate neuronal genomic instability, connecting proteostasis pathways to neuronal DNA repair.

Ubiquitin Biology
04

Neurodegeneration

Unresolved genomic instability in neurons initiates and accelerates neurodegeneration. We identify early biomarkers and druggable targets.

Therapeutic Translation
